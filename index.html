Pillow Fight Guide

Welcome to your ultimate pillow fight guide!

Step 1: Gather Your Pillows

Collect as many pillows as you can. The more, the merrier! Here are some types you might consider:

  • Soft pillows for a gentle touch
  • Decorative pillows for extra flair
  • Firm pillows for a more intense battle

Make sure they are clean and fluffy!

Step 2: Choose Your Battle Area

Pick a safe and spacious area for your pillow fight. Here are some suggestions:

  • Your living room (clear out breakables!)
  • A bedroom with plenty of space
  • A large area with soft flooring (like a carpet)

Ensure that there are no obstacles that could cause injury.

Step 3: Set the Rules

Before starting, it's important to set some ground rules:

  • No hitting too hard!
  • Aim for the body, not the face.
  • Decide on a time limit or rounds.
  • Agree on a safe word to stop the fight if needed.

Having clear rules makes the fight more fun and safe!

Step 4: Start the Fight!

Now that you’re prepared, it’s time to start the pillow fight!

Remember to:

  • Have fun and enjoy the moment!
  • Follow the rules you set earlier.
  • Take breaks if needed.

Happy fighting!
